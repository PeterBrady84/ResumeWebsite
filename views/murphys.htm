Murphy’s bar is a family run pub located on Blessingtons Main Street. Located on the N81 route, Murphy’s provides food and drink to a busy passing trade aswell local residents of Blessington. Adjacent to the pub is Wonka's sweet shop which sells a wide range of old fashioned sweets as well as balloons and party decorations.
